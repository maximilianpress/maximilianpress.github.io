Science stuff

I did my graduate work and a short postdoc studying a grab bag of topics in genetics and genomics, including such random things as:

  1. Why some bacteria can gain a gene and others can't (my answer: not all of them remembered to upgrade their operating systems).
  2. Microsatellites are weird and probably more important than we want to admit.
  3. "Epistasis" is a word meaning "we don't understand the causal basis of any genotype-phenotype relationship".
  4. Plants grown at high temperatures are bad at telling time.
